Installation Guide

Since "installing" bookmarklet in the mobile version of Safari isn't as easy as in the original this guide should be of help.

  1. Bookmark this page by hitting the + on the bottom of your screen. Later this will be the bookmark to use the in page search.

  2. Tap the following textfield once to focus it and a second time so the Select all-bubble appears. Hit it! Then hit the Copy-button in the next bubble that appears.

  3. Go to your bookmarks and hit the Edit-button on the bottom left. Tap the bookmark you just created for this page.

  4. Tap the second field that contains the URL and clear it by hitting the X on the right. Tap the field again so the Paste-bubble appears. And paste you shall.

  5. Hit the blue Done-button on the bottom right. That's it. :)
